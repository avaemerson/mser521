Bears are cute.

Black bear paw print in mud thick as brownie batter if I never saw another person it probably wouldn’t matter

I used to bring bear spray but the bears then stayed away

Be wary of bears asking for directions

Trailhead signs point us where we need to go they warn us of bears and coyotes of water sources they let us know but they can’t take our pictures they’re only wood and steel if they could just capture those emotions soon-to-be-hikers can only feel
